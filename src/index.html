How to use theremover
You have to follow three simple steps

1. Upload

First, choose the image you want to remove background from by clicking on “Upload image”. Your image format can be PNG or JPG. We support all image dimensions.

2. Remove

Our tool automatically removes the background from your image. Next, you can choose a background color. Our most popular options are white and transparent backgrounds, but you can pick any color you like.

3. Download

And the last: you can download the resulting photo and compare with the original. Now you can use this photo anywhere and enjoy the result.
